COVID-19 Information COVID-19 The Virus COVID-19 is a new illness that can affect your lungs and airways. It is caused by a virus named "Coronavirus". Coronaviruses are a group of related diseases in mammals and birds. In humans, coronaviruses cause respiratory tract infections that can range from mild to lethal. The effects of COVID-19 The Symptoms It can take up to 14 days for symptoms of COVID-19 to show. These symptoms include: A high fever (38 degrees Celsius or above) A cough Shortness of breath If you experience these symptoms, it is requested that you sefl-isolate and contact your GP through mobile or online. How to protect yourself and others. General Advice Recommended practices: Wash hands frequently. Cover your mouth when coughing or sneezing. Immediately discard used tissues. Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth. Comply with requested movement restrictions unless absolutely neccessary.
